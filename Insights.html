FTT Insights – Data Exploration & Visualization

A sample data analysis project demonstrating structured data and visual insights.

Objective

  • Illustrate FTT’s approach to organizing and analyzing data.
  • Demonstrate visualization skills and analytical workflow.
  • Provide a sample framework that can be applied to more complex projects.

Dataset

This project uses a small, hypothetical dataset for demonstration purposes:

Run # Vehicle Tire Type Lap Time (s) Track Temp (°C) Notes
1 Car A Soft 72.4 28 Dry track
2 Car A Medium 74.1 28 Dry track
3 Car B Soft 73.0 29 Slight wind
4 Car B Medium 75.2 29 Slight wind

Analysis & Visualization

Charts and visualizations help identify trends and patterns in the dataset:

  • Line Chart: Track lap times across multiple runs.
  • Bar Chart: Compare performance by tire type.
  • Scatter Plot: Examine correlation between track temperature and lap performance.

Insights

  • Soft tires tend to yield faster lap times under dry conditions.
  • Track temperature affects lap time consistency, with higher temps slightly increasing variance.
  • Small environmental factors like wind can subtly influence results.

Key Takeaways

  • Structured data + clean visualization = actionable insights.
  • FTT’s process emphasizes clarity, reproducibility, and simplicity.
  • FTT Insights can serve as a template for real-world projects requiring data-driven decisions.
