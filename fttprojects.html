Current Projects

At Fish Tank Tech, we design, build, and innovate across software, analytics, photography tech, and motorsports systems. Here are 10 of the projects currently in development.

1. DPAC Timing Engine

The Dynamic Performance Adjustment Coefficient (DPAC) engine—our experimental motorsports normalizer—combines drivetrain configuration, power-to-weight potential, tire profile, and elevation compensation to create fair-adjusted race standings.

2. FTT Portfolio CMS

A lightweight, custom-built content system powering photography, motorsports, and corporate pages—optimized for speed, low maintenance, and cross-project syncing.

3. Activism Photography Booking Suite

A self-service photo session booking tool with automated availability, deposit handling, contract signing, and event delivery integration.

4. Activism Motorsports Driver Hub

A data-forward interface for drivers to view race results, car telemetry, DPAC-revised standings, and event-specific analytics.

5. FTT Fabrication Workflow Tracker

A digital management tool for fabrication projects—tracking parts, build stages, materials, and labor hours with a visual progress dashboard.

6. FTT Client Dashboard Framework

A reusable dashboard framework that lets clients track progress on development, media, or data deliverables—with messaging, revisions, and exports.

7. Fish Tank Tech API Core

A multi-service backend powering authentication, project sync, usage analytics, and internal micro-services used across FTT products.

8. Autonomous Timing Camera Prototype

A portable camera system for motorsports that uses visual timestamping and car motion profiles to generate backup timing verification.

9. FTT Asset Delivery Vault

High-capacity photo and video delivery system with expiration links, client galleries, watermark controls, and download tracking.

10. FTT Internal Development Toolkit

A suite of CLI and web-based tools to speed up building sites, APIs, and scripts—featuring template generators, environment automation, and deployment helpers.
